Company History

Amordad Investment and Trade Group, active since 2000 with over two decades of commercial and trading experience and offices in Tehran, Chabahar Free Zone, and the United Arab Emirates, is a specialized and pioneering company in importing and supplying stainless steel sheets, various carbon steel sheets, aluminum sheets and their products, supply of goods, sea freight, and more. The company aims to sustainably supply high-quality and reasonably priced raw materials and goods required by domestic industries. In recent years, the company has focused on supplying stainless steel sheets and their products according to international standards and competitive prices to end consumers, especially in petrochemical, refinery, home appliances, construction, automotive, pharmaceutical, and food industries. Stainless steel sheets are the company's main trade, and this strategic focus is key to its development. The company's strategy is: “Quality and reasonable price speak louder than advertising.” 

The company aims to expand its activities in the specialized field of stainless steel sheets by establishing close relationships with consuming industries and market players, striving to become your first choice among suppliers by providing high-quality products at competitive prices. 

Other business capabilities include supplying, shipping, ordering, and customs clearance of various goods needed by customers from top Asian and European suppliers based on customer orders. 

 

Services offered by Amordad Investment and Trade Group: 

1- Supply of stainless steel industrial sheets (grades 321, 316, 304, 430...), carbon steel sheets (ST37, ST52, X60), aluminum sheets and their products, stocked in Tehran warehouse and delivered nationwide.  2- Consulting and providing services for purchasing, shipping, ordering, transit, and customs clearance of various steel, stainless steel sheets and their products.  3- Supply of various goods based on customer orders from top Asian and European suppliers, door to door.  4- Competitive pricing of goods in FOB, CIF, and CFR terms.
